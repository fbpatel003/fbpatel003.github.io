Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Overview

TGDrive ("we", "our", or "the application") is a browser-based cloud storage interface that allows users to store and manage files using their own Telegram account via the official Telegram MTProto API. TGDrive operates entirely in your browser and has no backend server. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our own servers.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

Because TGDrive has no backend server, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Your Telegram API credentials (API ID or API Hash)
  • Your Telegram session tokens or authentication keys
  • Any files you upload or download through TGDrive
  • Usage analytics or behavioural data
  • IP addresses or device identifiers

3. Data Stored Locally in Your Browser

TGDrive stores the following exclusively in your browser's IndexedDB. This data never leaves your device:

  • Telegram API ID and API Hash — stored locally to authenticate with Telegram
  • Telegram session string and authentication keys — maintains login across refreshes
  • File and folder metadata cache — filenames, sizes, MIME types for faster navigation
  • Image thumbnails — cached locally, auto-evicted after 30 days or 200 entries

Clear all local data any time via browser site settings. Clearing logs you out but files on Telegram remain unaffected.

4. Telegram API Usage

TGDrive communicates directly with Telegram's servers via encrypted WebSocket using the MTProto protocol. Your files and credentials flow directly between your browser and Telegram — TGDrive is not an intermediary. This is governed by Telegram's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. TGDrive is not affiliated with Telegram Messenger Inc.

5. Google AdSense and Advertising Cookies

TGDrive displays advertisements via Google AdSense. AdSense uses cookies to serve personalised ads based on your browsing history. You may opt out at Google Ads Settings. See how Google uses data when you visit partner sites.

6. Adsterra Advertising

TGDrive also displays advertisements via Adsterra network. Adsterra may use cookies and tracking technologies to serve relevant ads. Their data practices are governed by Adsterra's Privacy Policy.

7. Cookies

TGDrive itself stores only one item in localStorage: your cookie consent preference. Advertising cookies from Google AdSense and Adsterra are third-party cookies managed by those services.

8. Children's Privacy

TGDrive is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes are posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of TGDrive constitutes acceptance.
