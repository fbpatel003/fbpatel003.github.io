Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Overview

TGDrive ("we", "our", or "the application") is a browser-based cloud storage interface that allows users to store and manage files using their own Telegram account via the official Telegram MTProto API. TGDrive operates entirely in your browser and has no backend server. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our own servers.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

Because TGDrive has no backend server, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Your Telegram API credentials (API ID or API Hash)
  • Your Telegram session tokens or authentication keys
  • Any files you upload or download through TGDrive
  • Usage analytics or behavioural data
  • IP addresses or device identifiers
  • Browsing history or activity logs

3. Data Stored Locally in Your Browser

TGDrive stores the following data exclusively in your browser's IndexedDB (a local browser database). This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to TGDrive servers:

  • Telegram API ID and API Hash — provided by you, stored locally to authenticate with Telegram's servers
  • Telegram session string and authentication keys — used to maintain your login session across page refreshes without requiring you to re-enter credentials
  • File and folder metadata cache — filenames, file sizes, MIME types, and upload timestamps cached locally for faster navigation
  • Image thumbnails — small preview images generated from your uploaded images, cached locally and automatically evicted after 30 days or when the cache exceeds 200 entries

You can clear all locally stored data at any time through your browser's site data settings for this domain. Clearing this data will log you out of TGDrive, but your files on Telegram's servers remain completely unaffected and accessible after re-authentication.

4. Telegram API Usage

TGDrive communicates directly with Telegram's servers using the MTProto protocol via encrypted WebSocket connections. Your files, credentials, and session data are transmitted directly between your browser and Telegram's infrastructure. TGDrive is not an intermediary in this communication.

This communication is governed by Telegram's Privacy Policy and Telegram's Terms of Service. TGDrive is an independent application and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially supported by Telegram Messenger Inc.

5. Google AdSense and Advertising Cookies

TGDrive displays advertisements served by Google AdSense on the drive interface pages. Google AdSense uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to serve personalised advertisements based on your visits to this and other websites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and other sites. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or www.aboutads.info.

For more information on how Google uses data when you use our site, see: How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.

6. Cookies

TGDrive itself uses only one item of browser local storage: a cookie consent preference stored in localStorage to remember whether you have accepted or declined advertising cookies. This preference is stored locally in your browser and is not transmitted anywhere.

Advertising cookies set by Google AdSense are third-party cookies managed entirely by Google. See Google's cookie policy for details on what these cookies contain and how long they persist.

7. Children's Privacy

TGDrive is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through our service, please contact us and we will take steps to remove such information.

8. Third-Party Links

TGDrive contains links to external websites including my.telegram.org, Telegram's documentation, and GitHub. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

9. Security

Because TGDrive has no backend server, there is no TGDrive database that could be breached. The primary security consideration is the security of your browser device and your Telegram account. We strongly recommend enabling two-factor authentication on your Telegram account and keeping your API credentials confidential.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal and regulatory reasons. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top. Your continued use of TGDrive after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
